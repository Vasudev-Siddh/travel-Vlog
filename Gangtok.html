Gangtok, Sikkim


Embark on a journey to Gangtok, the capital and largest town of the Indian state of Sikkim. Join us on a virtual tour through the vibrant monasteries, scenic landscapes, and cultural delights that make Gangtok a captivating destination nestled in the Eastern Himalayas.

Gangtok Overview

Explore the unique features and cultural richness of Gangtok:

  • Enchey Monastery: Visit the serene Enchey Monastery, offering panoramic views of Gangtok and the Kanchenjunga range.
  • MG Marg: Stroll along MG Marg, the lively and pedestrian-friendly street known for its shops, eateries, and vibrant atmosphere.
  • Nathula Pass: Explore the Nathula Pass, a high-altitude mountain pass that connects India with China.

Monasteries and Spiritual Retreats


Immerse yourself in the spiritual and cultural heritage of Gangtok:

  • Rumtek Monastery: Visit the Rumtek Monastery, a key center for the Kagyu sect of Tibetan Buddhism.
  • Ranka Monastery: Explore the peaceful Ranka Monastery, surrounded by lush greenery and offering meditation retreats.
  • Do-Drul Chorten: Pay your respects at the Do-Drul Chorten, a stupa with intricate religious carvings.

Nature and Adventure

Experience the natural beauty and adventure in and around Gangtok:

  • Tsomgo Lake: Discover the stunning Tsomgo Lake, a glacial lake surrounded by mountains and open grassland.
  • Banjakri Falls: Visit the Banjakri Falls, a hidden gem nestled in a forested area.
  • Paragliding in Gangtok: For adventure seekers, consider paragliding to enjoy breathtaking aerial views.

Local Cuisine


Savor the flavors of Gangtok's unique cuisine:

Try local dishes like Phagshapa, Thukpa, and Momos, and experience the blend of Tibetan and Sikkimese influences.

What to Wear and Pack

Plan your attire and essentials for a comfortable stay in Gangtok:

  • Layered Clothing: Due to varying temperatures, pack layers, including a jacket and warm clothing.
  • Comfortable Shoes: Wear comfortable shoes for exploring monasteries and the town.
  • Rain Gear: Given the region's occasional rainfall, carry a rain jacket and umbrella.
  • Camera: Capture the scenic landscapes and cultural moments of Gangtok.

How to Reach Gangtok


Plan your journey to Gangtok with these transportation options:

  • By Road: Gangtok is well-connected by road, and buses and taxis are available from nearby cities.
  • By Air: The nearest airport is Bagdogra Airport, and a taxi or bus can take you to Gangtok.
  • By Rail: The nearest major railway station is New Jalpaiguri (NJP), and you can continue by road to Gangtok.

Responsible Travel Tips


Contribute to the preservation of Gangtok's beauty with these responsible travel tips:

  • Respect Local Culture: Follow local customs and traditions, especially when visiting monasteries.
  • Minimize Environmental Impact: Dispose of waste responsibly and avoid littering in natural areas.
  • Support Local Economy: Choose local accommodations and support local businesses and artisans.
