Munnar, Kerala


Embark on a journey to Munnar, a breathtaking hill station in the Western Ghats of Kerala, India. Join us on a virtual tour through the lush tea plantations, misty mountains, and serene landscapes that make Munnar a paradise for nature lovers and tranquility seekers.

Munnar Overview

Explore the unique features and natural beauty of Munnar:

  • Tea Plantations: Wander through the vast tea plantations of Munnar, witnessing the lush greenery and the process of tea cultivation.
  • Eravikulam National Park: Discover the biodiversity of Eravikulam National Park, home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr.
  • Mattupetty Dam: Enjoy the scenic views and boating at Mattupetty Dam, surrounded by hills and forests.

Scenic Spots and Attractions


Immerse yourself in the scenic beauty and attractions of Munnar:

  • Top Station: Visit Top Station for panoramic views of the Western Ghats and the neighboring state of Tamil Nadu.
  • Attukal Waterfalls: Experience the beauty of Attukal Waterfalls, surrounded by lush green landscapes.
  • Pothamedu Viewpoint: Enjoy the breathtaking views of Munnar from Pothamedu Viewpoint.

Nature and Adventure


Experience the natural beauty and adventure in and around Munnar:

  • Chinnar Wildlife Sanctuary: Explore the wildlife and diverse ecosystems of Chinnar Wildlife Sanctuary.
  • Anamudi Peak: Trek to Anamudi Peak, the highest peak in South India, for stunning views of the surroundings.
  • Tea Museum: Learn about the history of tea in the region at the Tea Museum in Munnar.

Local Cuisine


Savor the flavors of Munnar's unique cuisine:

Try local dishes like Kerala Sadya, Appam with Stew, and fresh banana chips.

What to Wear and Pack

Plan your attire and essentials for a comfortable stay in Munnar:

  • Comfortable Clothing: Pack light, comfortable clothing suitable for the cool climate of Munnar.
  • Walking Shoes: Wear comfortable walking shoes for exploring the hills and attractions.
  • Camera: Capture the picturesque landscapes and wildlife of Munnar.

How to Reach Munnar


Plan your journey to Munnar with these transportation options:

  • By Air: The nearest airport is Cochin International Airport, and you can hire a taxi or take a bus to Munnar.
  • By Road: Munnar is well-connected by road, and buses and taxis operate from major cities in Kerala.
  • By Rail: The nearest railway station is Aluva or Ernakulam, and you can continue your journey to Munnar by road.

Responsible Travel Tips

Contribute to the preservation of Munnar's beauty with these responsible travel tips:

  • Respect Nature: Follow the designated trails and avoid disturbing the flora and fauna.
  • Support Local Communities: Choose accommodations and services that support local communities and sustainable tourism.
  • Minimize Environmental Impact: Dispose of waste responsibly and avoid littering in natural areas.
