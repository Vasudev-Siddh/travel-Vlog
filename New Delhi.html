New Delhi, Delhi


Embark on a journey to New Delhi, the capital city of India, where ancient history and modernity coexist in harmony. Join us on a virtual tour through the iconic landmarks, diverse culture, and vibrant street life that make New Delhi a captivating destination.

New Delhi Overview

Explore the historical and cultural richness of New Delhi:

  • India Gate: Pay tribute to the soldiers at India Gate, a war memorial and iconic landmark.
  • Qutub Minar: Marvel at the UNESCO World Heritage Site, Qutub Minar, a towering masterpiece of Mughal architecture.
  • Lotus Temple: Visit the Lotus Temple, a Bahá'í House of Worship known for its stunning lotus-shaped architecture.

Cultural and Historical Landmarks

Immerse yourself in the cultural and historical treasures of New Delhi:

  • Red Fort: Explore the Red Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a symbol of India's rich history.
  • Jama Masjid: Visit the grand Jama Masjid, one of the largest mosques in India, with impressive Mughal architecture.
  • National Museum: Discover the diverse art and artifacts at the National Museum, showcasing India's heritage.

Arts and Crafts


Experience the vibrant arts and crafts scene in New Delhi:

  • Dilli Haat: Shop for traditional handicrafts, textiles, and regional delicacies at Dilli Haat.
  • National Handicrafts & Handlooms Museum: Explore the rich tapestry of India's handicrafts at this museum.

Culinary Delights

Savor the diverse flavors of New Delhi's culinary scene:

Indulge in street food delights like chaat and kebabs. Experience the richness of Mughlai cuisine and try local specialties like Butter Chicken and Chole Bhature.

Shopping in New Delhi


Discover the vibrant markets and shopping districts in New Delhi:

  • Connaught Place: Shop at Connaught Place for a mix of international brands and local boutiques.
  • Sarojini Nagar Market: Explore Sarojini Nagar Market for budget-friendly fashion and trendy accessories.

What to Wear and Bring


Plan your attire and essentials for a comfortable stay in New Delhi:

  • Modest Clothing: Respect local customs by wearing modest clothing, especially when visiting religious sites.
  • Comfortable Footwear: Wear comfortable shoes for exploring historical sites and bustling markets.
  • Reusable Water Bottle: Stay hydrated, especially in the warm weather of Delhi.
  • Camera: Capture the architectural beauty, cultural richness, and vibrant street scenes of New Delhi.

How to Reach New Delhi

Plan your journey to New Delhi with these transportation options:

  • By Air: Indira Gandhi International Airport is well-connected to major cities worldwide.
  • By Train: New Delhi Railway Station is a major railway hub with excellent connectivity.
  • By Metro: The Delhi Metro provides a convenient and efficient way to travel within the city.

Tips for Exploring New Delhi


Enhance your experience in New Delhi with these helpful tips:

  • Plan Ahead: Plan your itinerary and prioritize key attractions to make the most of your time.
  • Local Etiquette: Familiarize yourself with local customs and etiquette, especially when visiting religious sites.
  • Use Public Transport: Utilize the efficient public transport options like the Delhi Metro for hassle-free travel.
