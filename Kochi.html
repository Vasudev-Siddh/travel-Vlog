Kochi, Kerala


Embark on a journey to Kochi, a vibrant city in Kerala, India, known for its rich history, diverse culture, and picturesque landscapes. Join us on a virtual tour through the historical sites, cultural landmarks, and culinary delights that make Kochi a must-visit destination for travelers.

Kochi Overview

Explore the unique features and cultural richness of Kochi:

  • Fort Kochi: Wander through the historic streets of Fort Kochi, known for its colonial architecture, art galleries, and spice markets.
  • Chinese Fishing Nets: Witness the iconic Chinese Fishing Nets along the coast, a legacy of Kochi's maritime history.
  • Mattancherry Palace: Visit the Mattancherry Palace, showcasing exquisite murals and artifacts depicting the region's history.

Cultural and Historical Sites


Immerse yourself in the cultural and historical heritage of Kochi:

  • Jew Town: Explore Jew Town, home to the Paradesi Synagogue and a variety of antique shops.
  • St. Francis Church: Visit St. Francis Church, the oldest European church in India with historical significance.
  • Kerala Folklore Museum: Discover the vibrant traditions of Kerala at the Kerala Folklore Museum.

Nature and Scenic Beauty


Experience the natural beauty and scenic spots in and around Kochi:

  • Marine Drive: Enjoy a leisurely walk along Marine Drive, offering panoramic views of the backwaters and the city skyline.
  • Veegaland (Wonderla Kochi): Have a thrilling time at Veegaland, an amusement park with exciting rides and attractions.
  • Cherai Beach: Relax on the sandy shores of Cherai Beach, known for its serene atmosphere.

Local Cuisine

Savor the flavors of Kochi's unique cuisine:

Try local dishes like Karimeen Pollichathu (Pearl Spot Fish), Kerala Sadya (traditional feast), and Appam with Stew.

What to Wear and Pack


Plan your attire and essentials for a comfortable stay in Kochi:

  • Light and Comfortable Clothing: Kochi has a tropical climate, so pack breathable clothing.
  • Sunscreen and Hat: Protect yourself from the sun when exploring outdoor attractions.
  • Comfortable Footwear: Wear comfortable shoes for exploring the historical sites and markets.
  • Camera: Capture the vibrant colors and cultural moments of Kochi.

How to Reach Kochi

Plan your journey to Kochi with these transportation options:

  • By Air: Cochin International Airport is well-connected, and you can hire a taxi or use public transport to reach the city.
  • By Train: Ernakulam Junction is a major railway station, well-connected to major cities in India.
  • By Road: Kochi is accessible by road, and buses and taxis operate from nearby cities.

Responsible Travel Tips


Contribute to the preservation of Kochi's culture and environment with these responsible travel tips:

  • Respect Local Customs: Be mindful of local traditions and customs, especially when visiting religious sites.
  • Reduce Plastic Use: Minimize the use of plastic and dispose of waste responsibly to protect the environment.
  • Support Local Businesses: Choose locally-owned accommodations, restaurants, and shops to support the local economy.
