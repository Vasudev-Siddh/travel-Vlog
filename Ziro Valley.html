Ziro Valley, Arunachal Pradesh


Embark on a journey to Ziro Valley, a picturesque destination nestled in the state of Arunachal Pradesh, India. Join us on a virtual tour through the lush landscapes, vibrant culture, and unique traditions that make Ziro Valley a haven for nature lovers and cultural enthusiasts.

Ziro Valley Overview

Explore the unique features and cultural richness of Ziro Valley:

  • Talley Valley Wildlife Sanctuary: Discover the biodiversity of Talley Valley Wildlife Sanctuary, surrounded by lush greenery and scenic beauty.
  • Ziro Puto: Enjoy panoramic views of Ziro Valley from Ziro Puto, a vantage point offering breathtaking landscapes.
  • Apatani Village: Immerse yourself in the traditions of the Apatani tribe at an Apatani Village, known for its unique farming practices.

Cultural and Heritage Sites


Immerse yourself in the cultural and heritage richness of Ziro Valley:

  • Shiva Lingam at Kardo Forest: Visit the ancient Shiva Lingam at Kardo Forest, a sacred site surrounded by serene natural surroundings.
  • Siddheshwar Nath Temple: Explore the Siddheshwar Nath Temple, an important religious site in Ziro.
  • Local Handicrafts: Witness the craftsmanship of the locals and explore traditional handicrafts in Ziro.

Nature and Adventure


Experience the natural beauty and adventure in and around Ziro Valley:

  • Hike to Meghna Cave Temple: Take a trek to the Meghna Cave Temple for a combination of adventure and spirituality.
  • Pine Grove: Relax in the Pine Grove, a serene forested area offering a peaceful retreat.
  • Fishing at Kechopari Lake: Enjoy the tranquility of Kechopari Lake and indulge in fishing activities.

Local Cuisine

Savor the flavors of Ziro Valley's unique cuisine:

Try local dishes like Thukpa, Bamboo Shoot Curry, and Pika Pila (a fermented bamboo shoot chutney).

What to Wear and Pack

Plan your attire and essentials for a comfortable stay in Ziro Valley:

  • Comfortable Clothing: Pack comfortable and modest clothing suitable for the weather.
  • Good Walking Shoes: Wear sturdy walking shoes for exploring the scenic trails and villages.
  • Camera: Capture the natural beauty and cultural moments of Ziro Valley.

How to Reach Ziro Valley


Plan your journey to Ziro Valley with these transportation options:

  • By Air: The nearest airport is Tezpur Airport, and you can reach Ziro Valley by road from there.
  • By Road: Ziro is well-connected by road, and buses and taxis are available from nearby cities.
  • By Rail: The nearest railway station is Naharlagun Railway Station, and you can continue your journey to Ziro by road.

Responsible Travel Tips


Contribute to the preservation of Ziro Valley's beauty with these responsible travel tips:

  • Respect Local Traditions: Learn about and respect the cultural practices of the indigenous tribes.
  • Minimize Environmental Impact: Practice responsible tourism by minimizing waste and respecting nature.
  • Support Local Communities: Choose accommodations and services that support local communities and sustainable tourism.
