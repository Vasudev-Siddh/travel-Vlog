Hampi, Karnataka


Embark on a journey to Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site in Karnataka, India. Join us on a virtual tour through the ancient ruins, magnificent temples, and rich cultural heritage that make Hampi a captivating destination for history enthusiasts and explorers.

Hampi Overview

Explore the unique features and historical significance of Hampi:

  • Virupaksha Temple: Visit the iconic Virupaksha Temple, dedicated to Lord Shiva, and witness the vibrant and living traditions.
  • Hampi Bazaar: Wander through Hampi Bazaar, surrounded by ancient structures and traditional markets.
  • Vijaya Vittala Temple: Marvel at the intricate stone chariot and musical pillars of Vijaya Vittala Temple.

Historical Sites and Landmarks


Immerse yourself in the historical sites and landmarks of Hampi:

  • Lotus Palace: Explore the beautiful Lotus Palace, known for its delicate architecture and lotus-shaped structures.
  • Elephant Stables: Witness the grandeur of the Elephant Stables, once used to house royal elephants.
  • Queen's Bath: Visit the Queen's Bath, an ancient royal bathing complex with ornate architecture.

Natural Beauty and Scenic Spots

Experience the natural beauty and scenic spots in and around Hampi:

  • Hemakuta Hill: Climb Hemakuta Hill for panoramic views of the Hampi ruins and surrounding landscapes.
  • Tungabhadra River: Enjoy a peaceful riverside stroll along the Tungabhadra River, offering a serene escape.
  • Matanga Hill: Hike to the top of Matanga Hill for a breathtaking sunrise or sunset view.

Local Cuisine


Savor the flavors of Hampi's unique cuisine:

Try local dishes like Bisi Bele Bath, Jolada Roti with Brinjal Curry, and Holige (a sweet flatbread).

What to Wear and Pack

Plan your attire and essentials for a comfortable exploration of Hampi:

  • Comfortable Clothing: Wear light, comfortable clothing suitable for exploring historical sites.
  • Sun Protection: Carry a hat, sunglasses, and sunscreen to protect yourself from the sun.
  • Comfortable Footwear: Wear sturdy and comfortable footwear for walking through rocky terrains.
  • Camera: Capture the historical grandeur and scenic landscapes of Hampi.

How to Reach Hampi


Plan your journey to Hampi with these transportation options:

  • By Road: Hampi is well-connected by road, and buses and taxis operate from various cities in Karnataka.
  • By Train: The nearest railway station is Hospet Junction, and you can reach Hampi by road from there.
  • By Air: The nearest airport is Hubli Airport, and you can hire a taxi or take a bus to Hampi.

Responsible Travel Tips


Contribute to the preservation of Hampi's heritage with these responsible travel tips:

  • Respect Historical Sites: Follow designated paths, avoid touching or climbing on ancient structures, and refrain from littering.
  • Support Local Artisans: Consider purchasing locally crafted souvenirs to support the community.
  • Minimize Environmental Impact: Dispose of waste responsibly and respect the natural surroundings.
