Tawang, Arunachal Pradesh


Embark on a journey to Tawang, a serene destination nestled in the eastern Himalayas. Join us on a virtual exploration of the breathtaking landscapes, ancient monasteries, and cultural richness that make Tawang a haven for peace-seekers and adventure enthusiasts alike.

Tawang Overview

Discover the unique features and cultural significance of Tawang:

  • Tawang Monastery: Explore the largest monastery in India and a revered seat of Mahayana Buddhism.
  • Sela Pass: Witness the stunning landscapes and pristine beauty at Sela Pass, one of the highest motorable passes in the world.
  • Nuranang Falls: Marvel at the cascading beauty of Nuranang Falls amidst lush greenery.

Monastic Heritage


Immerse yourself in the spiritual and cultural heritage of Tawang:

  • Tawang Monastery: Visit the ancient Tawang Monastery, home to valuable scriptures and a towering statue of Buddha.
  • Galden Namgey Lhatse: Explore the Galden Namgey Lhatse Monastery, another significant Buddhist site in Tawang.
  • Thub-Chog Gatsel Ling Monastery: Discover the serene Thub-Chog Gatsel Ling Monastery, known for its tranquility and religious teachings.

Nature and Adventure

Experience the natural beauty and adventure in Tawang:

  • Tawang War Memorial: Pay homage to the brave soldiers at the Tawang War Memorial, offering panoramic views of the Tawang-Chu Valley.
  • Gorichen Peak: For adventure seekers, consider trekking to Gorichen Peak for panoramic views of the Himalayas.
  • Pankang Teng Tso Lake: Enjoy the tranquility of Pankang Teng Tso Lake, surrounded by snow-capped peaks.

Local Cuisine


Savor the flavors of Tawang's unique cuisine:

Try local dishes like Thukpa, Gyapa Khazi, and Momos, and experience the warmth of traditional Monpa hospitality.

What to Wear and Pack

Plan your attire and essentials for a comfortable stay in Tawang:

  • Warm Clothing: Due to the cool climate, pack layers, including jackets and thermals.
  • Comfortable Footwear: Wear sturdy shoes suitable for exploring monasteries and trekking trails.
  • Sunscreen and Sunglasses: Protect yourself from the high-altitude sun.
  • Camera: Capture the scenic beauty and cultural richness of Tawang.

How to Reach Tawang


Plan your journey to Tawang with these transportation options:

  • By Road: Tawang is well-connected by road, and buses and taxis are available from Tezpur and Bomdila.
  • By Air: The nearest airport is Salonibari Airport in Tezpur, with flights from major cities.
  • By Shared Taxis: Shared taxis are available for a scenic drive from Tezpur to Tawang.

Responsible Travel Tips


Contribute to the preservation of Tawang's beauty with these responsible travel tips:

  • Respect Local Customs: Follow the cultural norms and show respect when visiting monasteries and local communities.
  • Minimize Environmental Impact: Practice responsible trekking and dispose of waste properly.
  • Support Local Businesses: Choose local accommodations and support community-based tourism initiatives.
