Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a stunning white marble mausoleum in Agra, India, built by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Jaipur

Jaipur, the Pink City of Rajasthan, captivates with its regal palaces, vibrant markets, and rich cultural heritage.

Varanasi

Varanasi, the ancient spiritual city on the Ganges, is a cultural tapestry woven with ghats, Hindu rituals, and timeless traditions.

Shimla

Shimla, nestled in the Himalayan foothills, is a charming hill station known for its colonial architecture, scenic beauty, and vibrant markets.

Rishikesh

Rishikesh, Himalayan haven by the Ganges, is a serene hub for yoga and spirituality, crowned by the iconic Laxman Jhula.

Ladakh

Ladakh, a high-altitude desert in India, enchants with its stark landscapes, ancient monasteries, and a unique blend of Tibetan culture.

Amritsar

Amritsar, in Punjab, is renowned for the Golden Temple, a spiritual and architectural masterpiece at the heart of Sikhism.

New Delhi

New Delhi, India's capital, boasts a rich tapestry of history and modernity, with iconic landmarks like India Gate and the Lotus Temple.
