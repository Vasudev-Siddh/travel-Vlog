Alappuzha, Kerala


Embark on a journey to Alappuzha, a picturesque town in Kerala, India, known for its tranquil backwaters, scenic landscapes, and vibrant culture. Join us on a virtual tour through the winding canals, lush greenery, and cultural experiences that make Alappuzha a unique destination for travelers seeking serenity and natural beauty.

Alappuzha Overview

Explore the unique features and cultural richness of Alappuzha:

  • Alappuzha Backwaters: Cruise through the enchanting backwaters of Alappuzha, also known as the Venice of the East.
  • Alappuzha Beach: Relax on the golden sands of Alappuzha Beach and witness the breathtaking sunset.
  • Nehru Trophy Snake Boat Race: Experience the thrill of the annual Nehru Trophy Snake Boat Race, a traditional event drawing participants and spectators from across the globe.

Houseboats and Canals


Immerse yourself in the unique experience of houseboat stays and canal cruises in Alappuzha:

  • Houseboat Stay: Stay in a traditional Kerala houseboat and cruise through the serene backwaters, enjoying the tranquility of the surroundings.
  • Punnamada Lake: Explore Punnamada Lake, one of the largest lakes in Kerala, known for its scenic beauty and houseboat cruises.
  • Canal Cruise: Take a leisurely cruise through the narrow canals, witnessing village life and lush paddy fields.

Cultural Delights


Experience the cultural richness of Alappuzha:

  • Alappuzha Sree Krishna Swamy Temple: Visit the ancient Sree Krishna Swamy Temple, known for its architectural beauty and religious significance.
  • St. Mary's Forane Church: Explore St. Mary's Forane Church, a historic church with impressive art and architecture.
  • Alappuzha Mullakkal Chirappu: Attend the Mullakkal Chirappu festival, a grand celebration at Mullakkal Temple with cultural performances and rituals.

Local Cuisine

Savor the flavors of Alappuzha's unique cuisine:

Try local dishes like Karimeen Pollichathu (Pearl Spot Fish), Kerala Sadya (traditional feast), and Alleppey Fish Curry to experience the culinary delights of the region.

What to Wear and Pack


Plan your attire and essentials for a comfortable stay in Alappuzha:

  • Light and Comfortable Clothing: Pack light, breathable clothing suitable for the tropical climate.
  • Mosquito Repellent: Carry mosquito repellent, especially if you plan to stay near backwaters.
  • Camera: Capture the serene landscapes, traditional architecture, and cultural moments in Alappuzha.

How to Reach Alappuzha


Plan your journey to Alappuzha with these transportation options:

  • By Air: Cochin International Airport is the nearest airport, and you can hire a taxi or use public transport to reach Alappuzha.
  • By Train: Alappuzha Railway Station is well-connected, with regular trains from major cities in India.
  • By Road: Alappuzha is accessible by road, and buses and taxis operate from nearby cities.

Responsible Travel Tips

Contribute to the preservation of Alappuzha's beauty with these responsible travel tips:

  • Respect Local Environment: Avoid littering and respect the natural surroundings of the backwaters.
  • Support Local Economy: Choose eco-friendly accommodations and support local businesses for souvenirs and meals.
  • Follow Cultural Etiquette: Be mindful of local customs and traditions, especially when visiting religious sites.
