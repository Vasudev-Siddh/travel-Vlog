Taj Mahal,Uttar Pradesh


Step into the world of architectural splendor and historical significance at the Taj Mahal, a masterpiece that stands as an eternal symbol of love. Join us on a virtual journey through the enchanting history, stunning architecture, and cultural richness of the Taj Mahal.

Taj Mahal Overview

Learn about the marvels of the Taj Mahal:

  • Symbol of Love: Commissioned by Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his beloved wife, Mumtaz Mahal.
  • Architectural Marvel: Explore the intricate details of white marble and exquisite craftsmanship.
  • UNESCO World Heritage Site: Recognized for its cultural and historical significance on a global scale.

Visiting the Taj Mahal


Plan your visit to this iconic monument:

  • Opening Hours: The Taj Mahal is open from sunrise to sunset, except on Fridays.
  • Entrance Fees: Check the official website for the latest information on ticket prices and guided tours.
  • Best Time to Visit: Early morning and late afternoon provide ideal lighting for photography.

Architectural Wonders

Marvel at the architectural details of the Taj Mahal:

  • Main Mausoleum: Housing the tombs of Shah Jahan and Mumtaz Mahal.
  • Minarets and Mosque: Explore the four minarets and the adjacent red sandstone mosque and jawab.
  • Charbagh Garden: Experience the symmetry of the Persian-style garden surrounding the Taj Mahal.

Local Cuisine and Shopping


Indulge in the local flavors and unique shopping experiences:

Try Mughlai delicacies in nearby eateries. Explore local markets for traditional crafts, marble artifacts, and souvenirs.

What to Wear and Bring

Dress comfortably and prepare for your visit:

  • Modest Attire: Respect the cultural norms by wearing modest clothing.
  • Comfortable Shoes: Expect a fair amount of walking, so wear comfortable footwear.
  • Camera: Capture the awe-inspiring moments and architectural details.
  • Water and Snacks: Stay hydrated and energized during your exploration.

Guided Tours and Educational Programs


Enhance your understanding of the Taj Mahal through guided tours and educational programs:

  • Official Guides: Consider hiring an official guide to provide in-depth insights into the history and significance of each structure.
  • Educational Workshops: Check for educational programs or workshops that may be available for a deeper cultural experience.

Nearby Attractions

Explore other attractions in the vicinity of the Taj Mahal:

  • Agra Fort: Discover the historical Agra Fort with its impressive architecture and rich history.
  • Fatehpur Sikri: Visit the UNESCO World Heritage Site of Fatehpur Sikri, a city built by Emperor Akbar.

Photography Tips


Capture the beauty of the Taj Mahal with these photography tips:

  • Golden Hour Shots: Take advantage of the soft, warm light during the golden hours of sunrise and sunset.
  • Angles and Perspectives: Experiment with different angles to capture unique perspectives of the Taj Mahal.
  • Reflections: Utilize the reflective pools for stunning shots that include reflections of the monument.
