Kaziranga National Park

Kaziranga National Park in Assam, a UNESCO site, is celebrated for its stunning landscapes and successful conservation of the one-horned rhinoceros.

Meghalaya

Meghalaya, a land of living root bridges and perpetual rain, captivates with its lush landscapes and unique cultural tapestry.

Tawang

Tawang in Arunachal Pradesh, India, mesmerizes with its majestic landscapes and hosts the renowned Tawang Monastery, one of the largest in the world.

Gangtok

Gangtok, nestled in the Himalayas, is a captivating blend of panoramic vistas, Tibetan-Buddhist culture, and vibrant markets.

Kohima

Kohima, in Nagaland, is steeped in history, hosting the poignant War Cemetery and offering a glimpse into the rich cultural tapestry of Naga tribes.

Majuli

Majuli, the world's largest river island in Assam, is a serene haven where Vaishnavite Satras and lush landscapes create a unique tapestry of culture and nature.

Aizawl

Aizawl, the capital of Mizoram, charms with its scenic beauty, vibrant markets, and the harmonious blend of traditional Mizo culture and modern influences.

Ziro Valley

Ziro Valley in Arunachal Pradesh charms with scenic landscapes, rice fields, and the vibrant Ziro Music Festival, a fusion of nature and culture.
