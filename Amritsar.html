Amritsar, Punjab


Embark on a journey to Amritsar, a city that epitomizes the vibrant culture and rich history of Punjab. Join us on a virtual exploration of the iconic Golden Temple, historical sites, delicious cuisine, and heartwarming hospitality that make Amritsar a must-visit destination.

Amritsar Overview

Uncover the cultural treasures and historical significance of Amritsar:

  • The Golden Temple: Visit the holiest shrine in Sikhism, a symbol of spiritual enlightenment and architectural splendor.
  • Jallianwala Bagh: Reflect on the poignant history of the Jallianwala Bagh massacre at this public garden.
  • Wagah Border: Witness the lively and patriotic Wagah Border ceremony between India and Pakistan.

The Golden Temple


Immerse yourself in the spirituality and beauty of the Golden Temple:

  • Darbar Sahib: Pay your respects at the main shrine, surrounded by the serene Amrit Sarovar.
  • Akhal Takht: Explore the Akal Takht, the highest temporal seat of Sikh authority.
  • Langar Experience: Participate in the communal kitchen service and enjoy a humble meal at the Langar.

Historical and Cultural Sites

Discover the rich history and vibrant culture of Amritsar at these sites:

  • Maharaja Ranjit Singh Museum: Learn about the life and reign of the great Maharaja Ranjit Singh.
  • Gobindgarh Fort: Explore the historic Gobindgarh Fort, witnessing its museums and cultural shows.
  • Ram Bagh Gardens: Relax in the lush greenery of Ram Bagh Gardens, featuring a summer palace built by Maharaja Ranjit Singh.

Local Cuisine and Street Food


Savor the flavors of Amritsari cuisine and indulge in delectable street food:

Try iconic dishes like Amritsari Kulcha, Makki di Roti with Sarson da Saag, and end your meal with a glass of sweet Lassi.

Shopping in Amritsar

Explore the vibrant markets of Amritsar for traditional crafts and souvenirs:

  • Hall Bazaar: Shop for traditional Phulkari embroidery, Punjabi juttis, and handicrafts.
  • Lawrence Road: Discover modern shopping centers and boutiques for contemporary fashion.

What to Wear and Bring


Plan your attire and essentials for a comfortable stay in Amritsar:

  • Modest Clothing: Respect local customs by wearing modest clothing, especially when visiting religious sites.
  • Comfortable Footwear: Wear comfortable shoes for exploring historical sites and bustling markets.
  • Camera: Capture the architectural beauty, cultural richness, and vibrant street scenes of Amritsar.
  • Power Bank: Keep your devices charged for photography and navigation.

How to Reach Amritsar

Plan your journey to Amritsar with these transportation options:

  • By Train: Amritsar Junction is a major railway station well-connected to various cities.
  • By Air: Sri Guru Ram Dass Jee International Airport connects Amritsar to international and domestic destinations.
  • By Road: Amritsar is well-connected by road, and buses and taxis are available for convenient travel.

Tips for a Memorable Visit


Enhance your Amritsar experience with these tips:

  • Respect Religious Customs: Follow the guidelines and customs when visiting religious sites.
  • Experience Local Festivals: If possible, plan your visit during festivals like Baisakhi to witness vibrant celebrations.
  • Engage with Locals: Connect with the warm-hearted locals to learn more about the culture and traditions of Amritsar.
