Aizawl, Mizoram


Embark on a journey to Aizawl, the capital and largest city of the Indian state of Mizoram. Join us on a virtual tour through the picturesque landscapes, vibrant culture, and unique traditions that make Aizawl a captivating destination in the Northeastern region of India.

Aizawl Overview

Explore the unique features and cultural richness of Aizawl:

  • Durtlang Hills: Enjoy panoramic views of Aizawl from Durtlang Hills, offering a stunning backdrop of the city.
  • Mizo Village: Experience the traditional Mizo way of life in a Mizo Village, where you can witness indigenous customs and crafts.
  • Aizawl Zoological Park: Discover the diverse flora and fauna at Aizawl Zoological Park.

Cultural and Historical Attractions


Immerse yourself in the cultural and historical richness of Aizawl:

  • Mizoram State Museum: Explore the Mizoram State Museum, showcasing the cultural heritage and artifacts of the Mizos.
  • Solomon's Temple: Visit Solomon's Temple, an architectural marvel and a prominent landmark in Aizawl.
  • Vantawang Falls: Witness the beauty of Vantawang Falls, one of the highest waterfalls in Mizoram.

Nature and Adventure

Experience the natural beauty and adventure in and around Aizawl:

  • Bung Picnic Spot: Enjoy a peaceful picnic at Bung, surrounded by lush greenery and scenic landscapes.
  • Phawngpui Peak: Trek to Phawngpui Peak, the highest mountain in Mizoram, for breathtaking views.
  • Khawnglung Wildlife Sanctuary: Explore the biodiversity of Khawnglung Wildlife Sanctuary.

Local Cuisine


Savor the flavors of Aizawl's unique cuisine:

Try local dishes like Bamboo Shoot Curry, Bamboo Steamed Fish, and Mizo Vawksa (smoked pork curry).

What to Wear and Pack

Plan your attire and essentials for a comfortable stay in Aizawl:

  • Comfortable Clothing: Pack light, comfortable clothing suitable for the moderate climate.
  • Walking Shoes: Wear comfortable walking shoes for exploring the hilly terrain.
  • Rain Gear: Due to the region's occasional rainfall, carry a rain jacket and umbrella.
  • Camera: Capture the cultural richness and scenic landscapes of Aizawl.

How to Reach Aizawl


Plan your journey to Aizawl with these transportation options:

  • By Air: The nearest airport is Lengpui Airport, and you can reach Aizawl by taxi or bus.
  • By Road: Aizawl is well-connected by road, and buses and taxis operate from various cities.
  • By Rail: The nearest railway station is Silchar Railway Station, and you can continue your journey to Aizawl by road.

Responsible Travel Tips


Contribute to the preservation of Aizawl's beauty with these responsible travel tips:

  • Respect Local Customs: Follow the cultural norms and show respect when visiting religious sites and local communities.
  • Minimize Environmental Impact: Dispose of waste responsibly and avoid littering in natural areas.
  • Support Local Economy: Choose local accommodations and support local businesses and artisans.
